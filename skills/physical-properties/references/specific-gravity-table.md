## Using SG for Identification

### Quick Identification Tips

**Heavy stones (SG > 4.0)**:
- Zircon (4.6-4.8 high type)
- Almandine garnet (4.0-4.2)
- Corundum (4.0)
- Spessartine (4.1-4.2)

**Medium-heavy (SG 3.5-4.0)**:
- Spinel (3.6)
- Topaz (3.5)
- Chrysoberyl (3.7)
- Demantoid (3.8)

**Medium (SG 3.0-3.5)**:
- Tourmaline (3.0-3.1)
- Jadeite (3.3)
- Tanzanite (3.35)
- Peridot (3.3)

**Light (SG < 3.0)**:
- Beryl (2.7)
- Quartz (2.65)
- Feldspar (2.5-2.7)
- Opal (2.1)
- Nephrite (2.9-3.0)

### Heavy Liquid Reference

| Liquid | SG | Stones that Float | Stones that Sink |
|--------|-----|-------------------|------------------|
| Methylene Iodide | 3.32 | Beryl, Quartz, Opal, Feldspar | Corundum, Spinel, Garnet, Topaz |

**Note**: Many heavy liquids are toxic. Hydrostatic weighing is the preferred method.
