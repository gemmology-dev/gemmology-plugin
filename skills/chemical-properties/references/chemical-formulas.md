# Gemstone Chemical Formulas

Chemical compositions for major coloured gemstone species and varieties.

## Corundum Group

| Gemstone | Formula | Crystal System | Notes |
|----------|---------|----------------|-------|
| **Ruby** | Al₂O₃ | Trigonal | Cr³⁺ chromophore |
| **Blue Sapphire** | Al₂O₃ | Trigonal | Fe²⁺/Ti⁴⁺ charge transfer |
| **Yellow Sapphire** | Al₂O₃ | Trigonal | Fe³⁺ chromophore |
| **Padparadscha** | Al₂O₃ | Trigonal | Cr³⁺ + Fe³⁺ (colour centres) |
| **Pink Sapphire** | Al₂O₃ | Trigonal | Low Cr³⁺ content |

|----------|---------|----------------|-------|

| **Almandine** | Fe₃Al₂(SiO₄)₃ | Cubic | Fe²⁺ chromophore |
| **Spessartine** | Mn₃Al₂(SiO₄)₃ | Cubic | Mn²⁺ chromophore |
| **Rhodolite** | (Mg,Fe)₃Al₂(SiO₄)₃ | Cubic | Pyrope-almandine mix |

| **Malaia** | (Mg,Fe,Mn)₃Al₂(SiO₄)₃ | Cubic | Mixed composition |

## Notes on Chemical Notation

- Subscript numbers indicate atom ratios
- Parentheses indicate substitution (e.g., (Mg,Fe) = Mg or Fe)
- Chromophore = ion responsible for colour
- Superscript numbers indicate oxidation state (e.g., Cr³⁺)
- Water of crystallisation shown as ·nH₂O
