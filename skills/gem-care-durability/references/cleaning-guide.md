# Gemstone Cleaning Guide

Safe cleaning methods for coloured gemstones.

## Cleaning Method Categories

### Method 1: Warm Soapy Water (Safest)

**Suitable for**: Almost all gemstones

**Procedure**:
1. Use lukewarm water (not hot)
2. Add mild dish soap
3. Soak briefly (5-10 minutes)
4. Clean gently with soft brush
5. Rinse thoroughly
6. Dry with soft lint-free cloth

**Gems safe for this method**:
- Corundum (ruby, sapphire)
- Chrysoberyl
- Spinel
- Beryl (untreated)
- Topaz
- Tourmaline
- Garnet
- Quartz varieties
- Zircon

### Method 2: Ultrasonic Cleaning

**Suitable for**: Durable, untreated gems only

**Safe for ultrasonic**:
| Gemstone | Ultrasonic Safe | Notes |
|----------|-----------------|-------|
| Ruby (unheated/heat only) | Yes | No fracture filling |
| Sapphire (unheated/heat only) | Yes | No fracture filling |
| Spinel | Yes | |
| Diamond | Usually | Check for fractures |
| Quartz | Yes | |
| Chrysoberyl | Yes | |

**NOT safe for ultrasonic**:
| Gemstone | Reason |
|----------|--------|
| Emerald | Treatment damage |
| Glass-filled ruby | Filling damage |
| Tanzanite | Cleavage risk |
| Opal | Fragile, water content |
| Turquoise | Porous, treated |
| Pearl | Damage to nacre |
| Kunzite | Cleavage risk |
| Moonstone | Cleavage risk |
| Heavily included stones | May fracture |

### Method 3: Steam Cleaning

**Suitable for**: Very durable, stable gems only

**Safe for steam**:
| Gemstone | Steam Safe | Notes |
|----------|------------|-------|
| Ruby (untreated) | Yes | |
| Sapphire (untreated) | Yes | |
| Spinel | Yes | |
| Diamond | Usually | Check stability |

**NOT safe for steam**:
| Gemstone | Reason |
|----------|--------|
| Emerald | Treatment damage, inclusions |
| Any treated stone | May damage filler |
| Opal | Thermal shock |
| Tanzanite | Cleavage, thermal shock |
| Heat-sensitive gems | Temperature damage |
| Organic gems | Heat damage |

## Gemstone-Specific Guidelines

### Corundum (Ruby and Sapphire)

| Treatment Status | Warm Water | Ultrasonic | Steam |
|------------------|------------|------------|-------|
| Untreated | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| Heat only | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| Flux-healed | Yes | Caution | No |
| Glass-filled | Only | No | No |
| Diffused | Yes | Yes | Caution |

**Glass-filled ruby special care**:
- Warm soapy water ONLY
- No ultrasonic
- No steam
- Avoid acids (even lemon juice)
- Avoid heat

### Emerald

| Treatment Status | Warm Water | Ultrasonic | Steam |
|------------------|------------|------------|-------|
| Minor oil | Yes | No | No |
| Moderate oil | Yes (brief) | No | No |
| Resin filled | Yes (brief) | No | No |
| Significant fill | Caution | No | No |
| Untreated (rare) | Yes | Caution | No |

**Emerald care**:
- Warm soapy water, soft brush
- Avoid prolonged soaking
- No ultrasonic (any treatment)
- No steam (inclusions + treatment)
- Avoid solvents
- May need re-oiling over time

### Tanzanite

| Method | Safe | Notes |
|--------|------|-------|
| Warm water | Yes | Gentle only |
| Ultrasonic | No | Cleavage risk |
| Steam | No | Thermal shock |
| Brush | Soft only | No pressure |

**Special considerations**:
- Perfect cleavage = fragile
- Temperature changes risky
- Handle with care

### Opal

| Method | Safe | Notes |
|--------|------|-------|
| Warm water | Brief | Don't soak |
| Ultrasonic | No | Will damage |
| Steam | No | Will damage |
| Damp cloth | Best | Gentle wipe |

**Opal special care**:
- Avoid temperature changes
- Don't soak (especially Ethiopian)
- Keep away from chemicals
- Store with some humidity
- Avoid dry conditions

### Pearl

| Method | Safe | Notes |
|--------|------|-------|
| Damp cloth | Best | After each wear |
| Mild soap | Brief | Rinse immediately |
| Ultrasonic | No | Destroys nacre |
| Steam | No | Damages nacre |
| Chemicals | No | Acids dissolve nacre |

**Pearl special care**:
- Wipe after every wear
- Put on last, remove first
- Avoid perfume, hairspray, cosmetics
- Store separately (soft pouch)
- Restring periodically

### Turquoise

| Method | Safe | Notes |
|--------|------|-------|
| Damp cloth | Best | Gentle wipe |
| Warm water | Very brief | Pat dry immediately |
| Ultrasonic | No | Damage |
| Steam | No | Damage |
| Oils/chemicals | No | Absorbed |

**Turquoise care**:
- Porous - absorbs everything
- Avoid water exposure
- Avoid sunscreen, oils
- Stabilised more durable
- May change colour if untreated

### Organic Gems (Amber, Coral, Jet)

| Method | Safe | Notes |
|--------|------|-------|
| Damp cloth | Best | Gentle wipe |
| Warm water | Very brief | If needed |
| Ultrasonic | No | Damage |
| Steam | No | Heat damage |
| Chemicals | No | Damage |

**Special considerations**:
- All are soft
- All are heat-sensitive
- Amber may craze
- Coral sensitive to acids
- Store carefully

## Cleaning Frequency

### After Every Wear

| Gemstone | Action |
|----------|--------|
| Pearls | Wipe with soft cloth |
| Coral | Wipe gently |
| Amber | Wipe if needed |

### Weekly (Regular Wear)

| Gemstone | Action |
|----------|--------|
| Engagement rings | Warm soapy water |
| Frequently worn pieces | Appropriate cleaning |

### Monthly (Occasional Wear)

| Gemstone | Action |
|----------|--------|
| Most jewellery | Inspect and clean |
| Check settings | Look for loose stones |

### Annually (Professional)

| Service | Purpose |
|---------|---------|
| Professional cleaning | Deep clean |
| Setting inspection | Security check |
| Polish if needed | Restore finish |
| Check treatments | Emerald re-oiling etc. |

## Warning Signs

| Observation | Action |
|-------------|--------|
| Loose setting | Stop wearing, repair |
| Crack or chip | Assess damage |
| Colour change | Check for damage |
| Surface damage | Professional assessment |
| Treatment failing | Re-treatment needed |
